Introduction

This website caters to the needs of researchers involved in writing research articles in their own field of research. The very purpose of carrying out a research program is to master in the selected area of research and contribute new knowledge, data and findings. It is also a commitment for any researcher to give back his/her expertise to the literature from where he/she starts the research work. Publication of research articles in high impact factor journals helps the researchers to climb the ladder of their profession. International journal publications are considered as indicators of the quality of research. Many institutions around the world give significant weightage for high quality papers during appraisal programs. The online lectures proposed through this website will help the researchers to organize their research data, identify the research focus and organize the paper to be submitted to a selected journal. Guidelines for selection of suitable journal for publication will also be provided in these lectures.

Lectures focused on

Data and results

  • Organization of research data and extracting results
  • Preparation of tables and illustrations from results
  • Logical organization of results
  • Identification of research focus
  • Finalizing three to four core focal points

Paper writing

  • Overall idea of the research paper
  • Flow chart for logical sequence of the paper
  • Getting the template of the journal to be communicated
  • Organizing different parts of the paper
  • First draft, second draft and final paper

Communication with the journal

  • Submission of the article to the Editor of the journal
  • Follow up on the progress of review of the paper
  • Handling the review comments by the reviewers
  • Revising the paper after review comments
  • Resubmission of paper with rebuttals

Who can attend

  • Research scholars doing their experimental/numerical/theoretical research in educational institutions.
  • Scientists pursuing their research programs from research laboratories/organizations.
  • Anyone interested in writing research articles.
